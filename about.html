Building Meridion Systems

I am a software engineer with 7+ years building backend systems in C# and .NET. I spent years at Northrop Grumman contributing to flight simulation software, satellite program support, and mission planning systems, then moved into financial technology.

I founded Meridion Systems to build the infrastructure layer that serious retail traders and trading communities need: robust execution engines, systematic signal generation, and the connective tissue between alert and trade. The goal is a modular platform where signal sources, screening engines, and broker integrations are all independently swappable.

Vela is running live on paper. Spyglass is generating signals. The foundation is built. The work now is expanding the ecosystem.

I am completing a Master of Science in Computer Science with a focus on Artificial Intelligence at Georgia Tech, and hold a B.S. in Computer Science from UC San Diego.
